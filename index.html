2

3

Choose action by clicking or using arrow keys + Enter

4

Search actions by typing in the search box

5

Auto-insert processed text automatically

⚡

GPT Quick Query

How it works:

Type gpt: your question here in any text field
Press ⌘+Shift+G to process the query
Get response automatically appended to your text

Example queries:

gpt: What are the benefits of meditation?
gpt: How do I center a div in CSS?
GPT: Explain quantum computing in simple terms
gpt: Write a professional email declining a meeting
